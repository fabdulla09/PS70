<br> #### Week 2: Press Fit <p1> I wasnted to make a press fit kit out of one of my favorite shapes, which is a pentagon. This proved to be harder than inititally anticipated as originally I didn't account for the thickness of the material I was using <p1> <img src="pent.png" alt="guitar image" style="width: 500px; height: 500px;"> <p1> The image above was my first attempt at making the pentagon shape. I created it using Fusion360, but when I first created it I made it such that the slots were 2mm in length even though my material thickness was around 4mm. Thus pentagons that I originally made did not slot together that well at all and I had to adjust the length of the slots made. <p1> <img src="Print.png" alt="guitar image" style="width: 500px; height: 500px;"> <p1> I then went on to change the length to 3.9mm to account for the extra bit of material that the laser cutter takes off with the kerf it has <p1>
